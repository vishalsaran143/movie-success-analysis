  ## 🎬 Directors Budget vs Revenue

<img width="1340" height="525" alt="budget_revenue" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/43494d66-739e-40fb-a07b-dad876a26e1a" />

**What it shows:**
- Budget vs revenue for top directors

**Insight:**
<br>
Anthony Russo & Joe Russo generate the highest average revenue (~2.7B), followed by James Cameron (~2.3B).
A key pattern is that each director delivers 4–5x revenue compared to their budgets, making them strong investment choices for studios.
Joss Whedon also shows strong ROI with a relatively comparable budget.
